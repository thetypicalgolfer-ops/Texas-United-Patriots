Where We
Draw the Line.

Texas United Patriots stands for the values that built this state and the principles that will save it. These are the issues we will not compromise on — not now, not ever.

school

Education & Parental Rights

Our schools exist to educate — not to indoctrinate. We will expose CRT in every rebranded form it hides behind. We will defend the right of parents to know, question, and direct their children's education. Gender ideology has no place in a first-grade classroom, and taxpayer-funded universities will not be sanctuaries for ideological coercion.

Priority: Tier One

gavel

State Sovereignty

Texas is not a branch office of Washington, D.C. The Tenth Amendment still means what it says. We back the Texas Sovereignty Act, support the Joint Legislative Committee on Constitutional Enforcement, and will nullify unconstitutional federal overreach — from bureaucratic mandates to runaway court rulings.

Priority: Tier One

local_police

Back the Blue. Secure the Border.

No state can be free if its citizens are not safe. We fully and unconditionally support Texas law enforcement and the National Guard troops standing on our southern border. We reject every effort to "catch and release" criminal aliens and every attempt to hamstring the officers who protect our families.

Priority: Tier One

home

End the Property Tax Squeeze

A home is a sanctuary, not a revenue stream. We will expand homestead exemptions, compress school tax rates, and fight toward the ultimate goal: eliminating the homestead property tax so that no Texan is ever "renting" their own home from the government.

Priority: Tier One

bolt

Energy Dominance

Texas oil, gas, and nuclear power the American economy. We reject ESG boycotts, defend our $300 billion energy sector from "woke" corporate activism, and demand a dependable grid that never leaves a Texas family freezing in the dark again.

Priority: Tier One

account_balance

Dismantle DEI in Public Institutions

Senate Bill 17 is the law. We will enforce it. Every taxpayer-funded university that continues to fund DEI programs under new labels will be held accountable through the Ombudsman's public complaint portal and, if needed, state auditor referrals.

Priority: Tier Two

family_restroom

Faith, Family & Life

The family is the irreplaceable foundation of a free society. We defend the unborn, protect religious liberty, and will not stand by while faith-based institutions are dragged into court for teaching what billions of Christians, Jews, and Muslims have always believed.

Priority: Tier Two

storefront

Small Business & Economic Freedom

Main Street built Texas. We will cut red tape that strangles job creators, reject federal schemes to centralize banking and commerce, and keep Texas the number-one state in America to start, scale, and sell a business.

Priority: Tier Two
