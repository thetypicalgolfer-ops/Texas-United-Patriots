Texas United Patriots is a statewide advocacy movement of everyday Texans who refuse to let our state, our families, or our freedoms be surrendered to the radical Left.

Our Story

Built by Texans. Accountable to Texans.

Texas United Patriots was founded by a coalition of veterans, small-business owners, pastors, teachers, and parents who saw one undeniable truth: the Lone Star State was under attack — from radical federal overreach, from corrupt corporate activism, and from a mainstream press determined to rewrite our values into their image.

We do not answer to Washington. We do not answer to Wall Street. We answer to the people who raise their kids here, pay their taxes here, and bury their parents here. That is the entire mission. That is the entire mandate.

We recruit. We report. We train. We mobilize. And when the election comes, we elect the kind of leaders Texas used to take for granted — principled, tough, plainspoken men and women who put faith, family, and freedom first.

How We Fight

Three Fronts. One Mission.
